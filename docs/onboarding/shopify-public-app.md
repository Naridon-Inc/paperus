# Onboarding Guide: Shopify Public App

This guide explains how merchants can install and onboard Naridon from the Shopify App Store.

## Prerequisites
- An active Shopify store.
- Administrator access to the store.

## Installation Steps
1.  **Visit App Store Listing**: Go to the Naridon listing on the Shopify App Store (link pending).
2.  **Click Install**: Click the "Install" button.
3.  **Approve Permissions**: Review the requested permissions (read/write products, analytics, etc.) and click "Install app".
4.  **Redirect**: You will be redirected to the Naridon dashboard inside your Shopify Admin.

## Onboarding Flow
Once installed, the app will guide you through the setup:

1.  **Welcome**: Introduction to Naridon's features.
2.  **Product Selection (Auto)**: Naridon automatically scans your store's products. You don't need to do anything.
3.  **Competitor Setup**:
    *   Enter your top 3 competitors (domain names).
    *   Naridon can also auto-suggest competitors based on your product categories.
4.  **Prompt Generation**:
    *   The AI analyzes your products and brand name.
    *   It generates 5 "Golden Prompts" (questions potential customers might ask AI search engines).
    *   Review and approve these prompts to start tracking.
5.  **Dashboard**: You land on the main dashboard where initial analysis begins.

## Billing
-   **Free Plan**: You start on the Free plan automatically (3 competitors, 5 prompts).
-   **Upgrade**: Go to the "Pricing" tab to upgrade to Starter, Growth, or Pro via Shopify Billing.

## ✅ Deployment Checklist

### Naridon Team (Our Side)
- [ ] **App Store Listing**: Ensure app listing is live and up-to-date.
- [ ] **Support Setup**: Monitor Help Scout/Intercom for new user queries.
- [ ] **Analytics**: Verify PostHog/Google Analytics is tracking new installs.

### Customer Side
- [ ] **Install from App Store**: Find "Naridon" and click Install.
- [ ] **Approve Billing (Optional)**: If upgrading immediately, approve the Shopify recurring charge.
- [ ] **Configure Competitors**: Add at least 1 competitor domain.
- [ ] **Activate Autopilot (Optional)**: Enable auto-fix suggestions in Settings.

### Next Steps
1.  **Monitor Dashboard**: Watch for visibility score updates (daily).
2.  **Apply Fixes**: Review suggestions in the "Fix Engine" tab and apply them to products.
3.  **Upgrade Plan**: Move to a paid plan to unlock more prompts and competitors.
